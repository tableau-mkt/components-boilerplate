Content Reveal

Simple tool to make content hidden and revealed when a trigger is clicked. It doesn't matter where on the page the trigger and target content are, but the trigger element must target the content via a data-reveal-target="target-id" attribute. The .reveal--content container must have am id attribute that matches the data-reveal-target.

Example

Reveal Content
