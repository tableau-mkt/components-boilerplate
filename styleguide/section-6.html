Interactive Components (6.0)

This section displays examples of components that have interactivity. Most Interactivity is defined via Javascript.

Accordion (6.1)

An accordion of content that allows you to expand and collapse sections of content.

Examples:

This is an accordion item

Recusandae iste cupidatat provident hic incididunt inventore exercitationem habitant diam fugiat occaecat quod dignissim optio cum facilis sapiente! Autem pretium.

Another accordion item

Recusandae iste cupidatat provident hic incididunt inventore exercitationem habitant diam fugiat occaecat quod dignissim optio cum facilis sapiente! Autem pretium.

One more accordion item

Recusandae iste cupidatat provident hic incididunt inventore exercitationem habitant diam fugiat occaecat quod dignissim optio cum facilis sapiente! Autem pretium.

Markup:

<div class="accordion">
  <div class="accordion--item">
    <h3 class="accordion--item--title">This is an accordion item</h3>
    <div class="accordion--item--content">
      <p>Recusandae iste cupidatat provident hic incididunt inventore exercitationem habitant diam fugiat occaecat quod dignissim optio cum facilis sapiente! Autem pretium.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="accordion--item">
    <h3 class="accordion--item--title">Another accordion item</h3>
    <div class="accordion--item--content">
      <p>Recusandae iste cupidatat provident hic incididunt inventore exercitationem habitant diam fugiat occaecat quod dignissim optio cum facilis sapiente! Autem pretium.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="accordion--item">
    <h3 class="accordion--item--title">One more accordion item</h3>
    <div class="accordion--item--content">
      <p>Recusandae iste cupidatat provident hic incididunt inventore exercitationem habitant diam fugiat occaecat quod dignissim optio cum facilis sapiente! Autem pretium.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Sticky elements (6.2)

Uses waypoints.js and the sticky.js shortcut to make elements on the page stick once they get scrolled to and are about to go out of view.

Examples:

Default styling

Culpa doloribus cillum ac repellendus blandit elit illum! Eaque! Aenean excepturi conubia non eum, dolorum, quidem excepteur, quisque! Magni iaculis.

Doesn't add any spacing between the menu and the stuck element

.sticky--nospacing

Culpa doloribus cillum ac repellendus blandit elit illum! Eaque! Aenean excepturi conubia non eum, dolorum, quidem excepteur, quisque! Magni iaculis.

Markup:

<div class="grid-3">
  <div class="sticky {$modifiers}">
    <div class="box--green box__padded box__padded--one">
      <p>Culpa doloribus cillum ac repellendus blandit elit illum! Eaque! Aenean excepturi conubia non eum, dolorum, quidem excepteur, quisque! Magni iaculis.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Content Reveal (6.3)

Simple tool to make content hidden and revealed when a trigger is clicked. It doesn't matter where on the page the trigger and target content are, but the trigger element must target the content via a data-reveal-target="target-id" attribute. The .reveal--content container must have am id attribute that matches the data-reveal-target.

Examples:

Reveal Content
