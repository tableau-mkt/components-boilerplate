Font Families

BentonSans is primarily used for section headers and titling in it’s Thin case, but it is also used in it’s Book case in buttons and other scenarios. Occasionally BentonSans Medium is used in article templates.

Merriweather is used in it’s Light case mainly for body copy and in the Stories and Articles sections.
