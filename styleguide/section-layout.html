Layout

Components used for page layout

Grids

Used for creating a columned layout. To use these classes, just pick the number of columns wide you want your element to be relative to the total number of columns. The default is 12 columns. For example, you can use .grid--4 to make an element 4 columns wide followed by an element with .grid--8 to take up the remaining eight columns of the container's width.

Example

.grid--4
.grid--4
.grid--4
.grid--2
.grid--7
.grid--3

Markup:

<div class="clearfix">
  <div class="grid--4">
    <div class="box--desktop box__padded box__padded--one">
      <strong>.grid--4</strong>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="grid--4">
    <div class="box--desktop box__padded box__padded--one">
      <strong>.grid--4</strong>
    </div>
  </div>

    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Content Container

A wrapper elemnt to go around content to keep it in a centered column on the page.

Example

Officiis, per do porta platea sunt eligendi laboriosam, delectus vulputate quasi repellendus, incididunt consequat fames magnam. Nesciunt illum, gravida quis! Euismod. Nunc, nullam nemo ad erat habitant excepturi morbi? Voluptatum ligula, expedita eaque aliquam natus. Donec! Eu sodales ullam? Eos nec consequuntur, officia pariatur wisi ridiculus mollitia eget nam quasi.

Markup:

<div class="content-container">
  <p>
    Officiis, per do porta platea sunt eligendi laboriosam, delectus vulputate quasi repellendus, incididunt consequat fames magnam. Nesciunt illum, gravida quis! Euismod. Nunc, nullam nemo ad erat habitant excepturi morbi? Voluptatum ligula, expedita eaque aliquam natus. Donec! Eu sodales ullam? Eos nec consequuntur, officia pariatur wisi ridiculus mollitia eget nam quasi.
  </p>
</div>
