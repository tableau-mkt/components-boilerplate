Vertical Spacer

Used to create vertical spacing between sections of content without just a stack of <br> tags. Vertical spacers are made with horizontal rules (<hr>). The default spacing is 2em, but you can use modifier classes to add 4em or 6em of spacingas well. These examples put a spacer between two divs to dempnstrate the amount of space that will be added with each modifier.
